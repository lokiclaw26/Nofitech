## 5. UI/UX details worth calling out

- **Header is sticky** (`position: sticky; top: 0`) with a backdrop-blur so content scrolls under it cleanly.
- **Header pills** are updated client-side from `/api/version` + `/api/data/logs` + `/api/data/overview`:
  - `health: degraded` (amber) — driven by `app_health`
  - `project: mission-control` (blue) — from `overview.current_project.value`
  - `verified: 12h ago` (cyan) — from `logs.last_verification_rel`
  - `LAN · Real Data Only` (cyan, static)
  - `LAN: 192.168.0.29:8767` (gray, only visible in Detailed mode — Feature #7)
- **Last refreshed** lives in a thin meta strip directly under the header so it's always visible without crowding the pills.
- **Auto-refresh** is unchanged: `setInterval(loadAll, 30000)` and the initial `loadAll()` call are both present.
- **REFRESH button** retained its `onclick="loadAll()"`; styled to match the new topbar (dark panel, cyan hover).
- **Tasks table** now has `class="id"` on the ID column with monospace font and `white-space: nowrap`; status/priority pills are `white-space: nowrap`; other cells wrap normally.
- **Logs recent list** shows 5 events in a 4-column grid (time / level pill / officer / title) without source paths. Paths are tucked into a `<details>` "Show full log details" block that opens on demand.
- **Section headers** (h2) are now `text-transform: uppercase; letter-spacing: 0.05em` with a right-aligned `.hint` chip showing live counts (e.g. "3 cards", "real only", "1 real").
- **Responsive**: at ≤800px the topbar stacks vertically; at ≤600px the recent-list hides actor/level columns and the empty-state path becomes the layout's natural fallback.
