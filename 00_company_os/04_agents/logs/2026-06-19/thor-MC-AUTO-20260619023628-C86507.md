# 5 DIY Electronics Ideas: ESP32 + TFT Display

Innovative, fun, buildable on a maker bench. Mix of utility, art, and game. TFT = 2.8" ILI9341 or 3.5" ST7789 touch (the NOFI-favoured combo).

---

## 1. Wi-Fi Soulmate Plant Pot — "The Emotional Planter"

A smart pot that knows how your plant feels and shows its mood as a face on the TFT.

**Hardware:** ESP32-C3 Super Mini, 2.8" ILI9341 TFT, capacitive soil moisture sensor, BME280 (temp/humidity), small LiPo + TP4056.

**What it does:**
- Soil moisture + temp/humidity → drives an animated pixel-art face on the TFT
- Happy face + green cheeks when watered/sunny; droopy eyes + dry soil; sweaty face when hot
- Daily mood journal drawn on-screen as a comic strip (last 7 days)
- Sends "I need water" Telegram message via Wi-Fi

**Innovative bit:** Plants usually have boring dashboards. This one has *personality*. Kids and houseplant-killers love it.

**Build time:** Weekend. ~$25 BOM.

---

## 2. Pocket Cyber-Deck "Synth + Spectrum + Snake" — Triple Threat Gadget

A pocketable cyberdeck that boots into one of three modes via touchscreen, all on a single ESP32.

**Modes:**
- **Mini Synth:** On-screen piano keyboard, ESP32's I2S DAC (or PCM5102A) for output, 4-voice polyphony
- **Audio Spectrum Visualizer:** INMP441 mic → FFT → animated bars/waterfall on the 3.5" ST7789
- **Snake / 2048 / Pong:** Classic games with custom pixel art, high-score saved to NVS

**Hardware:** ESP32-WROOM, 3.5" ST7789 touch, PCM5102A DAC, INMP441 I2S mic, 3D-printed split clamshell case, 18650 battery.

**Innovative bit:** One device, three genuinely fun things to show off. The spectrum visualizer at parties, the synth for noodling, the games for nostalgia.

**Build time:** 2 weekends. ~$35 BOM.

---

## 3. Magic 8-Ball Reimagined — "Fortune 2.0"

The classic Magic 8-Ball, but with a TFT face that *watches you* and delivers deeply sarcastic AI-ish responses.

**Hardware:** ESP32-C3, 1.8" or 2.0" ST7789 TFT (small is better here), MPU6050 IMU (to detect shake/flip), small vibration motor, LiPo.

**What it does:**
- Shake to "ask" → animated swirling "thinking" animation → reveal answer
- 3 answer tiers: positive, neutral, negative — but written with a twist:
  - Positive: "The stars say yes, but my stars are LEDs, so grain of salt."
  - Negative: "Outlook hazy. Mostly because I'm a $3 chip."
- 200+ answers stored on-device, no cloud needed
- "Cursed mode" with answers from the NoFiTech Nofi lore

**Innovative bit:** Combines physical gesture (shake) with on-device personality. Zero-cloud, no accounts, fully offline. Perfect giftable hack.

**Build time:** 1 weekend. ~$15 BOM.

---

## 4. Live Train/Transit Departure Board — "My Commute, Pixels"

A real-time departure board for your local bus/train/metro, displayed on a desk TFT like a mini station platform.

**Hardware:** ESP32, 2.8" ILI9341 (or larger 4" if available), USB-C powered (desk-bound is fine).

**What it does:**
- Pulls from city open-data API / GTFS / WebSocket feed for the 2-3 nearest stops
- Renders scrolling departure list in green-on-black "flip-dot" font (using a bitmap font, not the default TFT font)
- Shows line color, destination, minutes-until, platform, delay icons
- Tap a stop on the touchscreen to switch which stop is being watched
- 4-color status: on-time (green), delayed (amber), cancelled (red), info (cyan)

**Innovative bit:** Turns the boring "what time is my bus" anxiety into a calm, glanceable display. Way cooler than checking your phone in a meeting. Optional: ambient light sensor dims display at night.

**Build time:** 1-2 weekends. ~$20 BOM.

---

## 5. The "Workout Referee" Fitness Coach — "Rep Counter That Roasts You"

A bench-press/deadlift spotter that watches your reps with a small camera and tells you — out loud and on-screen — when your form sucks.

**Hardware:** ESP32-S3 (has camera + AI acceleration), 2.8" TFT, OV2640 camera module on a small boom, INMP441 mic, PAM8403 amp + small speaker, LiPo.

**What it does:**
- ESP32-S3 runs a tiny pose-estimation model (e.g. **posenet-micro** quantized, or simple Haar-cascade for bar path)
- Counts reps, estimates tempo, watches bar path
- TFT shows: rep count, tempo bar, "FORM: GOOD / SHIT" with cute face
- Voice prompts: "Slow down on the descent", "Lockout your elbows", "Three more, you got this"
- 3 personalities: Drill Sergeant / Cheerleader / Sarcastic Robot

**Innovative bit:** Edge-AI in your gym bag. No cloud, no subscription, no monthly fee like a smart mirror. Privacy stays local. Genuinely useful for solo lifters.

**Build time:** 2-4 weekends (model + tuning). ~$40 BOM.
